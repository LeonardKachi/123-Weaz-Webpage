COMMON QUESTIONS

Everything You Need to Know

Simple answers to common questions about bespoke tailoring

What is bespoke tailoring?
Bespoke means a garment made entirely for you, from scratch. Unlike buying off the rack, we create a unique pattern based on your measurements and preferences. It's like having a piece of art made just for your body.
How long does it take?
Typically 6-8 weeks from consultation to completion. This includes multiple fittings to ensure perfection. Rush orders may be available depending on complexity.
Do I need to know my measurements?
Not at all! We'll guide you through the measurement process. You can visit our atelier for precise measurements, or we'll send you a simple guide if you're remote.
What if I don't know what I want?
That's perfectly fine! Our consultation is designed to understand your lifestyle, preferences, and needs. We'll guide you through options and help you discover what works best for you.
How much does bespoke cost?
Pricing varies based on garment type, fabric selection, and complexity. Our pieces typically range from $1,000 to $5,000+. We'll work within your budget to create something exceptional.
Can I see fabric samples?
Absolutely! We have an extensive library of premium fabrics from the world's finest mills. You can view samples during your consultation or we can send a curated selection based on your preferences.
